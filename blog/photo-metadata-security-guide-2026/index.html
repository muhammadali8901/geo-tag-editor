What is Photo Metadata?

Photo metadata is hidden information embedded within your image files that contains technical details about how and when the photo was taken. This data, stored in the EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) standard, can include:

📍 Location Data

  • • GPS coordinates (latitude/longitude)
  • • Altitude and bearing
  • • Street address (if available)
  • • City, state, country information

📷 Camera Information

  • • Camera make and model
  • • Lens information
  • • Focal length and aperture
  • • ISO speed and exposure time

📅 Date & Time

  • • Date and time taken
  • • Timezone information
  • • Date modified
  • • Digitization date

🔧 Technical Details

  • • Image resolution and dimensions
  • • File size and format
  • • Color space and compression
  • • Software used for editing

🚨 Privacy Risks of Photo Metadata

Critical Privacy Warning

Every time you share a photo online, you could be revealing your exact location, home address, workplace, and daily routines to strangers.

Real-World Privacy Breaches:

🏠 Home Location Exposure

Photos taken at home reveal your residential address, making you vulnerable to burglary and unwanted visitors.

💼 Workplace Tracking

Office photos can expose your workplace location, potentially revealing employment details to competitors or stalkers.

👶 Family Safety Risks

Children's photos with location data can reveal schools, playgrounds, and daily routines, creating serious safety concerns.

🏖️ Vacation Patterns

Travel photos create a timeline of your movements, indicating when your home is empty and revealing your lifestyle patterns.

2

Disable GPS on Your Device

Prevent location data from being recorded in the first place by disabling GPS for your camera app.

Quick Instructions:

  • • iPhone: Settings > Privacy > Location Services > Camera > Never
  • • Android: Settings > Apps > Camera > Permissions > Location > Deny
  • • DSLR: Check camera menu for GPS settings and disable
3

Use Privacy-Focused Apps

Some camera apps automatically strip metadata or give you control over what gets saved.

Recommended Apps:

  • • Signal: Automatically removes metadata from shared photos
  • • ProCamera: iOS app with metadata controls
  • • Open Camera: Android app with privacy features

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is removing metadata permanent?

Yes, once metadata is removed from a photo file, it cannot be recovered. Always keep a backup of the original file if you need the metadata later.

Does removing metadata affect image quality?

No, removing metadata does not affect the visual quality of your photos. Only the hidden data is removed.

Can social media platforms see my metadata?

Most social media platforms automatically strip metadata when you upload photos, but they may still collect some information. It's best to remove metadata yourself for maximum privacy.

Is your online tool really private?

Yes, our tool processes everything in your browser using JavaScript. Your photos never leave your device or get uploaded to any server.

What types of metadata can be removed?
