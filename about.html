About GeoTagPro

Making image geotagging accessible, fast, and private for everyone.

Our Mission

GeoTagPro was built with a simple mission: to provide the best free online tool for managing GPS metadata in digital photographs. We believe that everyone — from professional photographers to small business owners — should have easy access to powerful geotagging tools without needing to install expensive software or upload sensitive files to remote servers.

What We Do

Our Geo Tag Editor is a web-based tool that lets you add, edit, or remove GPS coordinates from JPEG images directly in your browser. Whether you need to geotag product photos for local SEO, correct inaccurate location data in your photo library, or remove sensitive GPS information before sharing images online, GeoTagPro handles it all.

Privacy First

What makes GeoTagPro different from other online tools is our commitment to privacy. Our entire tool runs client-side using JavaScript. This means your images are processed directly on your device and are never uploaded to any server. We never see, store, or have access to your photos. Your files stay on your computer from start to finish.

Why We Built This

We noticed that most existing geo tag editors were either outdated desktop applications, required software installations, or uploaded user images to remote servers for processing. Many tools had poor user interfaces, limited features, or charged fees for basic functionality.

We set out to build something better — a modern, fast, and completely free geo tag editor that works entirely in the browser. No downloads, no sign-ups, no hidden costs. Just open the page, upload your image, edit the GPS data, and download the result.
