Everything You Need for Image Geotagging

A complete set of tools for managing GPS metadata in your digital photographs.

Add GPS Location

Add precise GPS coordinates to photos that don't have location data. Perfect for photographers and businesses.

Edit Existing Geotag

Modify incorrect or outdated GPS coordinates in your images. Update latitude and longitude with precision.

Interactive Map Picker

Use the built-in OpenStreetMap to visually select a location. Click or drag the marker to set coordinates.

Remove Geotag for Privacy

Strip all GPS metadata from your images before sharing them online to protect your location privacy.

Read EXIF Metadata

View complete EXIF information including camera make, model, date, dimensions, and GPS data.

100% Client-Side

All processing happens in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server, ensuring total privacy.
