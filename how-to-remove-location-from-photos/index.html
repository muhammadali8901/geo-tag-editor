Why Remove Location from Photos?

Business Privacy

Privacy Tips

Additional Photo Privacy Tips

Best practices to protect your privacy when sharing photos online.

Disable GPS on Camera

Turn off location services on your smartphone camera to prevent photos from being geotagged automatically when you take them.

Check Social Media Settings

Review privacy settings on social media platforms. Some platforms automatically strip location data, while others may retain it.

Regular Privacy Checks

Make it a habit to check and remove location data from photos before sharing them online, especially when posting from identifiable locations.
