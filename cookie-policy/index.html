Geo Tags Editor Cookie Policy

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Geo Tags Editor (\"we\", \"us\", or \"our\") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website (https://geotagseditor.online). It helps you understand what tracking elements we deploy and how you can exercise control over them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, improve efficiency, and provide analytics reporting. Cookies set by us are called \"first-party cookies\". Cookies set by third parties are called \"third-party cookies\".

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use first-party and third-party cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons for our Website to operate, and we refer to these as \"essential\" or \"strictly necessary\" cookies. Other cookies enable us to track and target the interests of our users to enhance their overall on-page experience, or to serve targeted advertisements through Google AdSense.

3. Categories of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to navigate our site and use its core interactive tools (such as uploading files, map dragging, and file downloads). Without these, the core services of our online geotag editor cannot be provided. We also use a strictly necessary local storage key (gte_cookie_consent) to save your privacy settings choice.

3.2 Performance and Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous tracking statistics. This helps us understand how visitors interact with our pages, identify speed bottlenecks, and improve site performance. This data is fully aggregated and does not identify you personally.

3.3 Advertising Cookies (Google AdSense)

Google AdSense uses cookies to serve personalized advertisements to you based on your previous visits to our website or other sites on the Internet. These third-party cookies trace browsing activity to ensure relevant product display and limit the number of times you see a particular ad.

4. How to Manage and Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can exercise your preferences through our cookie consent interface or by adjusting your web browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings/privacy options.

4.1 Revoking Cookie Consent on Our Site

You can adjust or revoke your cookie choices at any time. Simply click the link in our footer to clear consent, or clear your browser's local storage and refresh the page to see the consent banner again.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please get in touch with us through our Contact Page.
