Geo Tags Editor Privacy Policy & Data Practices

Last Updated: May 16, 2026

At Geo Tags Editor, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our online geo tag editor tool.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Images and Files

Our geo tag editor tool processes images entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. No images or files are uploaded to our servers. All image processing, including reading EXIF metadata, modifying GPS coordinates, and generating the updated image, happens locally on your device. We have no access to any images you process using our tool.

1.2 Analytics Data

We may collect anonymous usage data through third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics. This data may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website
  • Approximate geographic location (country/city level, not precise GPS)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected in aggregate and does not identify individual users.

1.3 Advertising Data

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you. This may include:

  • Pages you visit on our site
  • Time spent on pages
  • Links you click
  • Ad interactions

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or by visiting www.aboutads.info.

1.4 Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics purposes and to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

1.5 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner is displayed asking for your permission to use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. Your consent preference is stored in your browser's local storage under the key gte_cookie_consent. No personal data is collected before consent is given. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or site data for geotagseditor.online.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the anonymous analytics data we collect to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website and tool
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns and trends
  • Ensure our website functions properly across different devices and browsers

3. Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis. Google Analytics may collect cookies and usage data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • OpenStreetMap: Our map feature loads map tiles from OpenStreetMap servers. Please refer to OpenStreetMap's Privacy Policy.
  • Content Delivery Networks (CDNs): We load JavaScript libraries from CDNs such as jsDelivr and unpkg to ensure fast loading times.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Since our tool processes all images client-side, your files never leave your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any images you process. Our website uses HTTPS encryption to protect data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of data collection
  • The right to data portability

Since we do not collect personal data through our tool, these rights primarily apply to any analytics data collected through your browsing activity.

6.1 GDPR (European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent UK/Swiss laws give you specific rights, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. Our legal basis for processing analytics and advertising data is your consent, which you give through the cookie consent banner and may withdraw at any time by clearing your browser's site data for geotagseditor.online.

6.2 CCPA / CPRA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) gives you the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, the right to correct inaccurate information, the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not knowingly sell personal information for monetary value. To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our Contact Page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Contact Page.
