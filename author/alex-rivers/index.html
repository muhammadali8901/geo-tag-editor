Alex Rivers

Lead Metadata Specialist & Photographer

Alex is a professional photographer and digital archivist with over 12 years of experience managing complex media libraries. His technical focus is on image metadata architecture, specifically the practical application of EXIF, IPTC, and GPS data for search visibility and privacy protection.

Expertise & Background

Alex's journey into the world of metadata began in commercial studio photography, where the need for precise asset organization led him to master command-line tools like ExifTool and professional management software. He soon realized that the most powerful tools were often the most difficult for the average user to access, leading to the creation of the browser-based Geo Tags Editor.

As the Lead Metadata Specialist at Geo Tags Editor, Alex reviews every guide and tutorial published on the site to ensure technical accuracy and practical relevance. He is a strong advocate for "Privacy by Architecture," pushing for client-side processing to keep sensitive location data in the hands of the users.
