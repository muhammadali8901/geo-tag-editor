## Cannibalization Guardrails
- Keep **transactional tool pages** focused on action intent (`add` vs `remove`) and avoid duplicating full “ultimate guide” copy.
- Keep **blog pages** intent-specific and internally link to the relevant tool page as the conversion destination.
- Avoid using the same exact-match phrase as dominant H1 on multiple pages unless intent is clearly different.

## Density Control Rules
- Cap exact-match primary keyword usage near 1.0-1.8% in body copy.
- Use semantic variants in headings and FAQs (EXIF data, photo location metadata, image privacy, coordinates).
- Prefer answer-first opening paragraphs for AI retrieval and featured snippets.
